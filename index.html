Whatever happened to the hole in the ozone layer?s
Người tổ chức Long Phạm | 1h 35m 34s
About this webinar Discover what caused the hole in the ozone layer, and how the Montreal Protocol helped restore it by banning harmful chemicals. What you’ll learn In the 1980s, the world faced a huge problem: there was a rapidly expanding hole in the ozone layer. If it continued to grow, rates of skin cancer could skyrocket, photosynthesis would be impaired, agricultural production would plummet, and entire ecosystems would collapse. So, what happened?
